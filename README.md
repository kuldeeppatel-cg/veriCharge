## 📌 Overview

**VeriCharge** is a high-performance platform engineered to eliminate range anxiety and streamline the electric vehicle charging experience. The application tackles the critical problem of EV charger availability by utilizing real-time geographic data to connect drivers with the most optimal, time-efficient charging stations.

## 🚀 The Problem We Solve

Finding an available EV charger can be frustrating and time-consuming. VeriCharge solves this through three core pillars:

1. **Intelligent Location Discovery**: Instantly scans your current geographic location to pinpoint the nearest operational EV charging stations around you.
2. **Predictive Availability Tracking**: Eliminates the frustration of arriving at a station to find all chargers occupied. VeriCharge tracks live queue data to show you exactly *which* chargers are currently free and predicts *when* occupied chargers will become available.
3. **Turn-by-Turn Navigation**: Once a station is selected, the platform seamlessly navigates the driver directly to their chosen charging bay using the most efficient route.
